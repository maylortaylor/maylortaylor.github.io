I am a highly skilled Full Stack Developer with 6+ years of experience in a real world work environment. I currently live in the St Petersburg, FL area and have been doing so since 2011. I got my start as an **IT Admin / Backend Developer** at a technology company in Seminole, FL where I was in charge of doing general IT work such as fixing employee computers and managing users. I eventually was able to gain enough knowledge at this company to help them with their SQL databases, Access databases, and any C# applications they needed to be developed. I made my way to a educational SaaS company in the fall of 2014. I was one of two developers for the first 2 years at this company. In those two years, we redesigned the company's applications to be forward-thinking. We designed a SQL / C# backend (using Dapper .NET for the ORM) with an AngularJS 1.6 frontend using .NETCORE's MVC framework. More recently (summer 2018) I have been learning technologies outside of my wheelhouse such as PHP (Laravel), Docker, and React.
